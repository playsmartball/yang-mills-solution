## ‚ùå What NOT TO Mention

### Never Include:
- "AI-assisted development"
- "ChatGPT verification"
- "LLM validation"
- "Claude identified error"
- Any reference to computational assistance in theory development

### Why Not:
1. **Scientific skepticism** - AI work is viewed with suspicion
2. **Undermines credibility** - Suggests you didn't do the work
3. **Irrelevant** - Development tools don't validate mathematics
4. **Precedent** - No one acknowledges IDEs, compilers, calculators in detail

## üìä Presentation Hierarchy

2. **Physical predictions** - Do they match experiment?

- What tools you used
- How many iterations it took
- What computational aids you employed
