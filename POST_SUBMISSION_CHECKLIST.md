## Contingency Plans

### If Reception is Mixed

**Positive signals:**
- High download count
- Technical questions (not dismissals)
- Replication attempts
- Seminar invitations

**Concerning signals:**
- Fundamental errors identified
- No engagement from community
- Dismissive comments

**Response to criticism:**
- Take all feedback seriously
- Distinguish technical errors from philosophical disagreements
- Update arXiv with corrections if needed
- Consider publishing in specialized journal first

### If Major Issue Found

**Do NOT:**
- Become defensive
- Dismiss criticism
- Claim conspiracy

**Do:**
- Thank the person for finding it
- Assess severity honestly
- Post correction to arXiv if minor
- Withdraw and fix if major
- Science self-corrects - embrace this
